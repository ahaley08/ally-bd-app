🔒 Correctional Center Sourcing Google Powered Search
Google-powered search — NPI Registry does not reliably categorize correctional facilities. Use the queries below to find jails, prisons, and detention centers seeking behavioral health partnerships, then log targets directly to your pipeline.
💡 Search Tips & Suggested Queries click to expand
Best query patterns:
• "[City/County] county jail behavioral health" — finds local jail BH contracts
• "[State] department of corrections mental health vendor" — finds state prison BH programs
• "correctional healthcare RFP behavioral health [state]" — finds active bid opportunities
• "[Company] correctional behavioral health" — research existing vendors (Wellpath, Centurion, NaphCare)

Key sources:
• State DOC websites (look for vendor/contractor pages)
• County Sheriff websites (medical/healthcare services)
• SAM.gov — federal procurement for BOP facilities
• Your state's eProcurement portal for active RFPs
📋 Log a Target to Pipeline Manually capture targets found via search
🎓 School Sourcing Google Powered Search
Google-powered search — NPI Registry returns dialysis and unrelated clinical facilities for school taxonomy codes. Use the queries below to find school districts and educational institutions seeking behavioral health partnerships.
💡 Search Tips & Suggested Queries click to expand
Best query patterns:
• "[County/City] schools mental health services" — finds district-level BH programs
• "[School district name] behavioral health vendor" — targets specific districts
• "[State] school mental health services grant" — finds funded programs seeking providers
• "school based health center behavioral health [city]" — finds school health centers

Key sources:
• State Dept of Education websites (mental health initiative pages)
• Individual school district websites (community partnerships section)
• SAMHSA School Mental Health resource directory
• Your state's student wellness or SEL initiative pages
• Local education agency (LEA) annual reports
📋 Log a Target to Pipeline Manually capture targets found via search
🏢 Employer Sourcing Google Powered Search
Google-powered search — Employers are not meaningfully represented in NPI Registry (pharmacy results dominate). Use the queries below to find large employers, HR leaders, and EAP opportunities, then log targets to your pipeline.
💡 Search Tips & Suggested Queries click to expand
Best query patterns:
• "[City] largest employers" — find top targets by employee count
• "[Company name] EAP provider" — research existing EAP relationships
• "[City] Chamber of Commerce members" — broad employer directory
• "self-insured employer behavioral health [state]" — high-value targets
• "[Industry] employee mental health program [city]" — industry-specific

Key sources:
• Local Chamber of Commerce member directories
• Business Journal "Book of Lists" (largest employers by metro)
• LinkedIn company search by location + employee count
• SHRM local chapter member directories
• State economic development agency employer databases
• Fortune 500 / Inc 5000 filtered by geography
